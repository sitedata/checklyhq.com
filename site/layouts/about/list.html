About Checkly

Checkly was founded in Berlin in 2018 to give the active monitoring and end-to-end-testing space a kick in the butt. We believe in a bright future where apps are built and shipped more reliably from dev to production and further.

Our vision is to create the active reliability platform for modern DevOps teams. A platform with world class developer experience!

How we work

We care about delivering an awesome service. We work on Checkly and open source projects like Puppeteer Recorder. We don't take ourselves too serious; we take our work and our customers very seriously.
